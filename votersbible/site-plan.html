Overview

Purpose

Have you ever wished you had one place to go to compare all the candidates running for offices stances on different platforms? This is the site for you. This website will compile all the candidates running for office and their opinions on the different matters. You will no longer be a that name sounds good voter. You will be educated.

Audience

My audience is residents of Utah (for now) who are citizens, 18 years of age and older. They would like to vote; I will have a link to voter registration. That way if they are not registered, they can do so. My audience wants to do what is best for this country and vote to the best of their knowledge. It is also for those who cannot yet vote but want to start familiarizing themselves with the candidates. They would like to know what they are talking about when they discuss politics.

Home page

Your Bible to Informed Voting

Welcome to Voters Bible, your go-to website for comprehensive and reliable information about candidates running for representative positions in Utah. We understand the importance of an informed electorate, and our platform aims to empower voters like you by offering a wealth of knowledge and insights. With this website, you can easily navigate through the political landscape, acquaint yourself with the candidates, and make informed decisions when casting your vote. Our Commitment to Utah Voters: As an unbiased source of information, we are committed to presenting an impartial overview of each candidate's background, experience, and stance on key issues. Our platform strives to foster transparency, allowing you to explore the qualifications and positions of candidates running for representative positions across Utah. By promoting an informed electorate, we hope to strengthen the democratic process and encourage active citizen participation. Detailed Candidate Profiles: On this website, you will find detailed profiles of the candidates running for representative positions in Utah. Each profile will highlight important aspects such as the candidate's educational background, professional experience, previous public service, and notable achievements. We aim to present these profiles in a user-friendly format, allowing you to easily compare and contrast the candidates' qualifications and ideals. In-Depth Issue Analysis: We believe that understanding a candidate's stance on key issues is vital to making an informed decision. Our website provides in-depth analysis of the positions taken by each candidate on a wide range of topics. Whether it's healthcare, education, environmental policies, or economic development, our platform will help you grasp the nuances of each candidate's views and their potential impact on Utah. Voter Eligibility: To participate in the democratic process, it's essential to meet certain criteria. Here are the general requirements to be eligible to vote in the United States: 1. Are you a U.S. citizen? 2. Do you meet your state’s residency requirements? 3. Are you 18 years old on or before Election Day? 4. Are you registered to vote by your state’s voter registration deadline? 5. Have you not been convicted of a felony that restricts your voting rights? 6. Have you not been deemed mentally incapable by the state? 7. (For presidential elections) Do you reside in the United States? If you answered "Yes" to all the eligibility requirements but need to register, please follow the link provided to register to vote: [Register to Vote](https://www.vote.gov). Identifying Your Federal Representatives: It's essential to know who represents you at the federal level. You can find out who your representatives are and how to contact them by visiting the following links: - [House of Representatives](https://www.house.gov/representatives/find-your-representative) - [Congress](https://www.congress.gov/members/find-your-member) By staying informed about your representatives, you can effectively engage with them on the issues that matter to you and ensure your voice is heard. Remember, democracy thrives when citizens actively participate in the electoral process. Explore our website, familiarize yourself with the candidates, and exercise your right to vote for a better future.
