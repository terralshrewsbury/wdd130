Voter Eligibility:

To participate in the democratic process, it's essential to meet certain criteria. Here are the general requirements to be eligible to vote in the United States:

  • Are you a U.S. citizen?
  • Do you meet your state’s residency requirements?
  • Are you 18 years old on or before Election Day?
  • Are you registered to vote by your state’s voter registration deadline?
  • Have you not been convicted of a felony that restricts your voting rights?
  • Have you not been deemed mentally incapable by the state?
  • Do you reside in the United States? (For presidential elections)
